FAQ

Common Questions

Do my staff need to download anything?

No. All they need is WhatsApp, which they already have. Sales are recorded by sending a normal text message to the business number.

What if my staff write differently each time?

The system understands natural language. Whether they write "sold 2 bags rice 10k" or "2 bags of rice for 10,000", it processes the entry correctly.

Can I use it for multiple shop branches?

Yes. You can assign different staff to different branches, and the dashboard shows performance per location, all from one account.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers and is only accessible through your authenticated account.

How do I get started?

Click "Get Started", send a first message to our WhatsApp bot, and follow the brief setup. It takes under two minutes.

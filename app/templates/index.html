FAQ

Common questions

Do my staff need to download anything?

No. All they need is WhatsApp, which they already have. Sales are recorded by sending a message.

What if staff write the message differently each time?

The system understands natural language. "Sold 2 bags rice 10k" works just as well as "2 bags of rice for 10,000".

Can I use it for multiple branches?

Yes. Assign staff to branches and see each location's performance from one dashboard.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. Data is stored on encrypted servers and accessible only through your authenticated account.

How do I get started?

Click "Get started", message our bot on WhatsApp, and follow the brief setup. It takes under two minutes.

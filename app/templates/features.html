Features

Everything you need to run a tighter business

SDCS Ledger gives you practical tools to record sales, monitor performance, and stay in control — without complicating how you already work.

Core Features

What SDCS Ledger Does

WhatsApp Sales Recording

Record sales by sending a message. Your staff don't need to learn new software — they just text the sale as they would normally describe it.

Batch Sales Entry

In a rush? Send multiple sales in one message. The system reads each line separately and records them all at once.

Live Sales Dashboard

A clean web dashboard that shows your total revenue, daily performance, average sale value, and full transaction history — updated as sales come in.

Staff Management

Add your sales staff to the system. Each sale they record is attributed to them, so you know who sold what without asking.

Return & Refund Tracking

If a customer returns a product, mark the transaction as returned directly from the dashboard. The sale is removed from revenue without deleting the audit record.

Date Filtering

Filter your sales by today, this week, this month, or any custom date range. Useful for weekly reviews and month-end summaries.

CSV & PDF Export

Download your transaction records as a spreadsheet or a formatted PDF report at any time. Share it with your accountant or keep it for your records.

Transaction Search

Search your ledger by item name, customer, or amount. Quickly find specific transactions without scrolling through the full list.

Multi-Branch Support

Manage multiple shop locations under one account. Each branch has its own staff, and all sales are visible on your unified dashboard.
